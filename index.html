Richard Spence

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Arizona. My interests include graph theory, approximation algorithms, and computational complexity theory. I received a B.S. in Mathematics with Computer Science (Course 18-C) from MIT in 2016. My advisor is Prof. Stephen Kobourov.
